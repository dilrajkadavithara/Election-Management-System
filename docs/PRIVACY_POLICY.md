## 1. Overview
The Election Intelligence Engine ("The Platform") is a technical tool designed for candidates and political organizations ("The Data Fiduciary") to manage and analyze voter data. We act as a **Data Processor** under the DPDP Act 2023.

## 2. Data We Process
We process data uploaded by the Data Fiduciary, including:
*   Official Voter List Information (Names, EPIC IDs, House Numbers).
*   Campaign Intelligence (Voter Leaning, Phone Numbers, Voting Probability).
*   System Audit Logs (Login timestamps, agent activity).

## 3. Purpose of Processing
The data is processed solely for:
*   Digitalization of official electoral rolls.
*   Campaign coordination and field volunteer management.
*   Targeted voter outreach and communication.

## 4. Data Sovereignty & Protection
*   **Ownership**: All voter data remains the absolute property of the Candidate/Party.
*   **Storage**: Data is hosted on secure Indian servers (Bangalore Region) to ensure data sovereignty.
*   **Encryption**: All sensitive intelligence and passwords are encrypted using industry-standard protocols (PBKDF2/HS256).

## 5. Third-Party Sharing
**We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade voter data.** Data is only exposed to:
*   Authorized users within your campaign hierarchy (subject to your Role-Based assignments).
*   Selected communication APIs (WhatsApp/SMS) if you choose to trigger broadcasts.

## 6. Data Subject Rights
In accordance with the DPDP Act 2023, the Candidate (Data Fiduciary) is responsible for fulfilling requests from data subjects (Voters). The Platform provides the tools to:
*   Update or correct voter records.
*   Mark voters for "Opt-Out" (DNC).
*   Delete intelligence data upon campaign conclusion.

## 7. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries or data deletion requests, contact the system administrator.
